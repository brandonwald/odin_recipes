Peanut Butter Shake

A delicious treat!

How could you not love this wonderful shake

What you need to make this shake!

  • Milk
  • Peanut Butter
  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Milk
  • Whipped Cream
  • Roasted Peanuts

Steps to take!

  1. PLACE milk and peanut butter in blender container. Cover and blend until smooth.
  2. ADD ice cream. Blend until smooth.
  3. TOP with whipped topping and chopped peanuts, if desired.
